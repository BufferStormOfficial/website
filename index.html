What we do

We digitalize businesses. We help small and large-scale businesses to stand out in this digital era.

Menu Digitalization

In this service, we create a website for the client where the photos of the menu cards of the hotels, restaurants, or cafes of the client are present on the website so that customers of the client can view the menu and order food, there will be a QR code on the customer table of the hotel, scanning this QR code will take the customer to the website we created. This service provides a contactless menu in this COVID-19 situation and the client's business also gets recognization due to it, this is good for marketing.

Upscaling Digital Presence

We do list your business location on google maps so more people can out to your business and we also do Search Engine Optimization(SEO), through SEO name of your business comes at the top search result. For example, if you have a hotel listed on google maps and someone in your area googles for "Hotels near me" then the name of your hotel will appear at the top search results, this will helps you to get more customers.
